The Problem

Why product research is broken

💸

📊

Opaque scoring — "Editor's Pick" badges appear with zero methodology behind them.

📅

🎭

The Insight

Decisions need a system

The best decisions aren't made by reading more articles. They're made by applying a consistent framework — weighting criteria that matter to you.
